Process

Always, I start with research, then move into rapid sketches. Pen and ink drawings followed by watercolor washes are the base for digital manipulation. The final art clearly reflects an illustrator’s hand, but is digital in delivery and flexibility.

About

With a background in visual journalism at Newsweek and an MFA in Illustration I am happy in the intersection of words and images. (More)

Recently I started a small company making mobile apps for children and got a degree in UX design, which brought product design and mobile into my workflow.
